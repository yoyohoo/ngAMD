Datepicker(Kendo.Datepicker)

Basic usage:

The elements:
datepicker: <input id="datepicker" value="2016/02/22" />
monthpicker: <input id="monthpicker" value="2016:February"/>
The controller:
$('#datepicker').kendoDatePicker({
  format: 'yyyy-MM-dd'
});
$("#monthpicker").kendoDatePicker({
  start: "year",
  depth: "year",
  format: "yyyy:MMMM"
});

Advanced usage:

The elements:
A(attribute): <div date-picker></div>
C(class): <div class="datePicker"></div>
E(element): <date-picker></date-picker>
The directive:
.directive('datePicker', function() {
return {
	restrict: 'ACE',
	link: function($scope, $els) {
		setTimeout.call(this, function() {
			$els.find('input').kendoDatePicker({
				format: 'yyyy/MM/dd'
			});
		});
	},
	template: '<input value="2016/02/19" />'
}
})

Tips: Prams start, end, depth limit the date region.
We can custom the date format with anyway we like, such as: "yy%MM%dd", "@_@ : yy -_-! MM ^_^ dd", and so on.
Date formats:

  • yyyy Parses a 4 digits year.

  • yy Parses a 2 digits year.

  • y Parses a year with 1, 2, 3, or 4 digits.

  • MMMM Parses the full name of a month.

  • MMM Parses the short name of a month.

  • MM Parses a numeric month.

  • M Parses a numeric month.

  • M! Parses a numeric month, but allowing an optional leading zero

  • dd Parses a numeric day.

  • d Parses a numeric day.

  • d! Parses a numeric day, but allowing an optional leading zero

  • EEEE Parses the full name of a day.

  • EEE Parses the short name of a day.

  • HH Parses a 24 hours time.

  • H Parses a 24 hours time.

  • hh Parses a 12 hours time.

  • h Parses a 12 hours time.

  • mm Parses the minutes.

  • m Parses the minutes.

  • sss Parses the milliseconds.

  • ss Parses the seconds.

  • s Parses the seconds.

  • a Parses a 12 hours time with AM/PM.

  • Z Parses the timezone offset in a signed 4 digit representation

  • ww Parses the week number

  • w Parses the week number

  • G, GG, GGG Parses the era (AD or BC)

  • GGGG Parses the long form of the era (Anno Domini or Before Christ)
